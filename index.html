Why Should You Join Our Academy?

Discover what makes Gaza Quran Academy unique and transformative

1

Our Mission – To Nurture Practicing Muslims

At the heart of our academy lies a clear and powerful mission:

"To nurture a generation of Muslims who not only know their faith but live by it."

We don't just teach Islamic knowledge — we cultivate character, instill values, and inspire action. Our vision is to raise Muslims who embody the teachings of Islam in every aspect of life — with sincerity, purpose, and unwavering commitment to Allah ﷻ.

Join us, and be a part of a movement to build a stronger, spiritually connected Ummah.

2

Learn the Quran, Hadith & Salah with Proper Tajweed and Clarity

Our academy is committed to helping students read and understand the Holy Quran, Hadith, Salah (prayer), and Islamic supplications with accurate pronunciation and proper Tajweed.

To achieve this goal, we have carefully selected a dedicated team of qualified Islamic teachers who specialize in:

  • Teaching the Holy Qur'an with Tajweed
  • Guiding the correct way to perform Salah
  • Explaining authentic Hadith with clarity
  • Helping students memorize and understand daily Duas
  • Building strong foundations in basic Islamic knowledge

We aim to develop not just readers, but practicing Muslims who live their lives in accordance with the teachings of Islam.

3

Physical Exercise

At our academy, we are committed to nurturing students not only spiritually and morally — but also physically. We believe that a healthy body strengthens one's ability to practice faith, face challenges, and contribute positively to society.

The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said:

"A strong believer is better and more beloved to Allah than a weak believer."
— (Sahih Muslim)

Inspired by this Hadith, we actively engage our students in physical activities to build strength, discipline, and resilience alongside their spiritual growth.

Therefore, any student who wishes to learn martial arts, taekwondo, or self-defense will be given the opportunity to do so.

This unique approach gives our academy a clear advantage over many others.

4

Why is the Academy Named "Gaza"?

The academy has been named "Gaza" to express our heartfelt solidarity and compassion with the oppressed Muslims of Gaza. This name is a reminder of their resilience and struggle for justice.

As a gesture of support, any child from among the victims of Gaza who wishes to study at our academy will be provided completely free education.

Moreover, we will also strive—within our means—to assist the victims of Gaza in whatever way possible.

5

Friendly & Flexible Fee Plan

Our academy is not just a commercial platform — it's a mission-driven initiative. That's why our fee structure is exceptionally friendly and flexible.

We strongly believe that no child should be deprived of quality education due to financial limitations. Each student is welcome to contribute according to their means, and we are committed to working together with families who truly wish to be part of this journey.

In short, affordability with dignity is at the heart of our fee plan.

What Sets Gaza Quran Academy Apart ?

At Gaza Academy, we believe in developing the complete Muslim - spiritually, mentally, and physically. This is what truly distinguishes us from other Islamic academies.

Our Martial Arts Program Includes:

  • Traditional Martial Arts - Building discipline and respect
  • Taekwondo Training - Developing focus and precision
  • Self-Defense Skills - Practical protection techniques
  • Physical Fitness - Building strength and endurance
  • Mental Discipline - Developing patience and perseverance

This unique combination of spiritual education with physical training creates well-rounded Muslims who are strong in both faith and body - ready to face the challenges of the modern world while staying true to Islamic values.

Our Programs

Comprehensive Islamic education programs for all levels

Qaida

Foundation course for beginners to learn Arabic alphabets, basic pronunciation, and reading skills.

  • Arabic alphabet recognition
  • Basic pronunciation rules
  • Letter joining and forms
  • Simple word reading

Qur'an (Nazra)

Learn to read the Holy Qur'an with proper Tajweed rules and beautiful recitation.

  • Proper Tajweed application
  • Makharij (pronunciation points)
  • Sifaat (characteristics)
  • Fluent Qur'an reading

Translation & Tafseer

Understand the meanings and interpretations of Qur'anic verses for deeper comprehension.

  • Verse-by-verse translation
  • Classical Tafseer study
  • Context and background
  • Practical application

Salah Training

Learn the correct way to perform daily prayers with proper method and understanding.

  • Prayer methods and positions
  • Essential duas and recitations
  • Prayer timings and rules
  • Spiritual significance

Physical Training

Martial arts, taekwondo, and self-defense training for physical and mental strength.

  • Martial arts training
  • Taekwondo techniques
  • Self-defense skills
  • Physical fitness

Character Building

Develop Islamic values, ethics, and character based on Qur'an and Sunnah.

  • Islamic values and ethics
  • Prophet's character study
  • Daily life application
  • Community involvement
